## 1. Executive Summary
**KaLaro** is a 2D indie simulation and community style game that gamifies urban sustainability. Rooted in the Filipino spirit of ***Bayanihan***, **KaLaro** serves as a blueprint for zero-waste living and inclusive education.

---


### 🌍 UN Sustainable Development Goals (UNSDG) Focus
This project directly advocates for:
* **Goal 14: Life Below Water** - By preventing coastal trash from entering the ocean and harming marine life.
* **Goal 15: Life on Land** - By maintaining clean, sustainable coastal ecosystems.
* **Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities** - By encouraging grassroots, community-led waste management ("Kabayanihan").

## 🎮 How to Play
<img width="1197" height="587" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fa351037-15d8-4139-987c-eeb5dada085a" />

The beach is covered in trash, and you only have **1 minute** to clean up as much as possible! 

* **Move:** Use the `W`, `A`, `S`, `D` keys to navigate the beach.
* **Collect:** Walk into trash to pick it up. Your paws are small, so you can only carry **up to 3 pieces of trash** at a time!
* **Dispose:** Run to the designated recycling/trash bins to drop off your stack and earn points.
* **Goal:** Clear as much trash as possible before the timer hits zero and get a highscore!

---

## ✨ Features & Mechanics

* **Top-Down Movement:** Smooth, 8-directional character movement with animated sprites.
* **Inventory Stacking System:** A dynamic carrying capacity that limits the player to 3 items at a time, requiring strategic pathing to the bins.
* **Real-Time Scoring & Timer:** A tense 3-minute countdown timer with a global scoring system that tracks your total environmental impact across scenes.
* **Immersive Audio:** Features dynamic sound effects including sand footsteps, inventory buzzers, satisfying drop-off sounds, and continuous background music.
* **Scene Transitions:** A full game loop including a Splash Screen, Main Menu, Gameplay Level, and Game Over/Score Screen.
